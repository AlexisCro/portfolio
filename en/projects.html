Realize as part of my reconversion, CasaVin is a web application realized with a team where I am the project manager.

Work in a team is a wonderful opportunity to deepen your knowledge through the sharing of skills and the implementation and understanding of development workflow.

The goal of this application is to provide a wine cellar management tool to individuals and to follow their wines (tasting follow-up, stock follow-up, location of the bottle within their cellar, ...). A second version of this application will involve professionals and allow them to manage their cellar as well as their billing. It is at this point that the application will take all its potential. We want to provide a tool to connect individuals and wine professionals, preferably local to individuals.

Thus we want to provide a tool that helps manage the cellar of individuals but also a tool that allows local consumption or the relationship of the local wine merchant with the individual. The wine merchant will then be able to make proposals and help the individual.

Methodology SCRUM / Agile.

Technologies used:

  • HTML
  • CSS / Bootstrap
  • Javascript / Stimulus / Hotwire
  • Ruby on Rails
  • PostgreSQL
